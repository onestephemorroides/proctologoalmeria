About Dr. Jaime Jorge Cerrudo

Dr. Jaime Jorge Cerrudo is a specialist in coloproctology — the medical and surgical discipline dedicated to conditions of the colon, rectum and anus. His focus is on minimally invasive techniques that resolve pathologies in a single visit to the clinic, without the need for hospital admission or general anaesthesia.

His practice is based at Clínica de San Pío in central Almería, where he sees both Spanish and English-speaking patients, including expatriates and tourists from across the Costa de Almería and the province. He is an active member of the main national scientific associations in his specialty.

For expatriates and international patients: consultations are available in English. All procedures are carried out at the clinic under local anaesthesia, without the need for hospital admission, allowing patients to return home on the same day.

Clinical philosophy

Why choose a minimally invasive approach?

The vast majority of coloproctological conditions can be resolved without surgery, without hospital admission and without general anaesthesia. In Dr. Jaime Jorge Cerrudo's practice, over 90% of patients referred for haemorrhoid surgery were successfully treated with an outpatient procedure at the clinic.

The goal is always to offer the most effective and least aggressive solution for each patient — a single visit, immediate recovery, and a return to normal daily life within hours. Every case is assessed individually to determine the most appropriate treatment.
