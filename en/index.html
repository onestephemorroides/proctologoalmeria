About the doctor

Dr. Jaime Jorge Cerrudo

Specialist in General and Digestive Surgery · Coloproctology

Dr. Jaime Jorge Cerrudo is a coloproctology specialist with over 13 years of experience treating anal and colorectal conditions. He is the director of the ONEstep® clinic at Clínica de San Pío in Almería, an instructor on the AEC Proctology Course, and a member of both the AEC and AECP.

He treats patients from across the Costa de Almería, including expats and tourists who need specialist care during their time in Spain. His clinic offers diagnosis and treatment in a single visit, without the need for hospital admission or general anaesthesia in the vast majority of cases.
